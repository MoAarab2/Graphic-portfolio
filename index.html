About me

Hello my name is Mohamed Aarab and I’m a graphic designer, illustrator, web-designer and fine artist. But this portfolio is focused on the graphic design only. Creating creative content is an ongoing journey for me in which I tend to keep surprising myself with fascinating visuals. My goal is to listen, reflect, develop customized concepts according to the Client’s desires and even surpassing their expectations. I do the work I do because I love it. I like the industry. I like the people I meet (most of the time). I like to make the world a better place. Hopefully we cross paths someday and we can make the world a better and more beautiful place together. I also like to keep up with personal projects as much as I can. If you have any ideas, projects or opportunities you’d like to discuss, please get in touch.
